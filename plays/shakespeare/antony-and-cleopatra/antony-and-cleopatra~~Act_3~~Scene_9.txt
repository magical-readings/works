          Antony And Cleopatra : Act 3 : Scene 9

SCENE IX. Another part of the plain.
  Direction: Enter MARK ANTONY and DOMITIUS ENOBARBUS
    MARK ANTONY
        1  Set we our squadrons on yond side o' the hill,
        2  In eye of Caesar's battle; from which place
        3  We may the number of the ships behold,
        4  And so proceed accordingly.
  Direction: Exeunt
